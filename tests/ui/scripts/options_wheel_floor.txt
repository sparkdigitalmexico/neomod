# pins the WHEEL FLOOR (regression caught live by WH 2026-06-12): a hovered scroll
# surface that declines the wheel (here: options_contents emptied by a no-match search,
# fits-content) must not let the gesture act on surfaces occluded by its layer. the
# populated songbrowser carousel sits beneath the panel; without the floor the scan falls
# through to the screen-wide carousel-forward claim and scrolls the OCCLUDED carousel
# (consumer elem=SongBrowser - verified pre-fix); with the floor the scan stops at the
# options group and only the fall-through sink may take it (consumer elem=VolumeOverlay,
# volume bar shows). the wheel point (300,288) is over options_contents AND over the
# (empty, stable-named) score browser region beneath - NOT over the carousel, whose
# occluded-hover button names would be selection-dependent (the known hover-gain P4 hole,
# cf. options_over_songbrowser).
options_save_on_back 0
debug_fixed_frametime 0.0166667
ui_trace 1
mouse_raw_input 0
mouse_sensitivity 1
ui_scale_to_dpi 0
windowed_resolution 1280x720
osu_folder uitest_osu_folder_full
mouse_to 10 10
@wait 30
set_active_ui_screen songbrowser
@wait 300
sendkey Left Ctrl down
@wait 3
sendkey O
@wait 3
sendkey Left Ctrl up
@wait 5
ui_assert visible optionsoverlay 1
sendtext nonexistentsearchstring
@wait 10
mouse_to 300 288
@wait 5
ui_assert hovered options_contents 1
mouse_wheel -1
@wait 5
ui_assert visible volumeoverlay 1
exit
